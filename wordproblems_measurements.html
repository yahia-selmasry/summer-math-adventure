ANSWER KEY

Page 1 – US Customary Length

1. 16 yards (48 ÷ 3 = 16)
2. Maya's room is wider by 6 inches (9 ft = 108 in; 108 − 102 = 6)
3. 21,120 feet (2 mi × 5,280 ft × 2 runs)
4. 7 spaces (7 yd = 21 ft; 21 ÷ 3 = 7 sections)

Page 1 – Weight & Capacity

5. 48 ounces (3 × 16)
6. 32 servings (2 gal × 16 cups)
7. 7 pounds 0 ounces (112 ÷ 16 = 7)
8. 12 bottles (6 qt × 2 pt = 12 pt)

Page 2 – Metric Units

9. 190 mm (19 × 10)
10. 1,400 meters (1.4 × 1,000)
11. 2.5 kg (2,500 ÷ 1,000)
12. 4 bottles (3,000 mL ÷ 750 mL)

Page 2 – Elapsed Time & Multi-Step

13. 7 hours 30 minutes
14. 8:35 p.m.
15. 245 cm (500 cm − 3 × 85 cm)
16. Dog weighs more by 100 oz (dog = 36×16+4 = 580 oz; cat = 488 oz; 580−488 = 92 oz) Note: dog wins by 92 oz

Challenge Problems

17. Start: 3 gal = 48 cups. Sold noon: 7 qt = 14 pt = 28 cups. Sold afternoon: 9 pt = 18 cups. Total sold: 28 + 18 = 46 cups. Left: 48 − 46 = 2 cups.

18. Board = 4 yd = 144 in. Two pieces of 2 ft 6 in = 30 in each → 2 × 30 = 60 in. Three pieces of 18 in → 3 × 18 = 54 in. Total cut: 60 + 54 = 114 in. Left: 144 − 114 = 30 inches.
