ROUNDING & ESTIMATION
Rounding Rule: Look at the digit ONE place to the RIGHT of where you're rounding.
• If it's 0–4 → round down (keep the digit the same, replace the rest with zeros)
• If it's 5–9 → round up (add 1 to the digit, replace the rest with zeros)

ADDITION & SUBTRACTION
Key Reminder: Always line up digits by place value before adding or subtracting.
When subtracting, if the top digit is smaller, you must regroup (borrow) from the next column.
Check: Addition answer − one addend = other addend.   Subtraction answer + subtracted number = starting number.
