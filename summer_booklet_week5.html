AREA & PERIMETER
Perimeter = total distance around the outside (add all sides). Units: cm, m, ft, in.
Area = space inside a shape. Units: cm², m², ft², in².
• Rectangle: A = length × width   P = 2(l + w)
• Triangle: A = 12 × base × height
• Parallelogram: A = base × height   (height is perpendicular distance, NOT the slanted side!)

Part A – Find Area & Perimeter

Shape 1 – Rectangle Perimeter = cm
Area = cm²
Shape 2 – Triangle Area = cm²
Shape 3 – Square Perimeter = m
Area = m²

VOLUME & COORDINATES
Volume = length × width × height. Units: cm³, m³, ft³, in³.
Think of it as: (base area) × height, or count cubic unit cubes.
Coordinate plane: (x, y) → x = right/left, y = up/down. Origin = (0,0).
Quadrant I: (+, +)   II: (−, +)   III: (−, −)   IV: (+, −)

Part A – Volume of Rectangular Prisms

Prism 1 V = l × w × h = __ × __ × __ = cm³
Prism 2 V = ft³

V = in³
