ANSWER KEY

Page 1 Answers

1. 109 = 1,000,000,000; 1,000 times bigger (109 ÷ 106 = 103 = 1,000)
2. 93,000,000 miles; Hours ≈ 93,000,000 ÷ 10,000 = 9,300 hours
3. 10,000 + 100,000 = 110,000 books; 100,000 building has more by 90,000
4. 10(10), 100(10²), 10,000(10⁴), 100,000(10⁵), 100,000,000(10⁸)
5. Table: 1, 2, 4, 8, 16, 32, 64; Powers: 2³, 2⁴, 2⁵, 2⁶; After 6 hrs: 64; 8 times more than hour 3 (64÷8)
6. Round 5 = 2⁵ = 32 people; Total = 1+2+4+8+16+32 = 63 people
Challenge: 2⁷ = 128 layers; Thickness = 128 × 0.1 = 12.8 mm = 1.28 cm

Page 2 Answers

7. Side = √144 = 12 m; Perimeter = 48 m
8. 64 squares; 8² = 64 (the total squares equal the base squared)
9. 9²=81; 12²=144; Difference = 63 more tiles
10. Side = 40÷4 = 10 ft; Area = 10² = 100 sq ft
11. Team A = 3⁴ = 81; Team B = 4³ = 64; Team A wins by 17 pts
12. Dungeon = 2⁶ = 64; Puzzle = 6² = 36; Dungeon gives 28 more coins
13. Earth is heavier; 10²⁴ > 10²² (Earth's power of 10 is larger)
14. Standard form: 0.000008 m; "eight millionths of a meter"
15. Storage = 300 GB; Projects = 300 ÷ 5 = 60 projects
16. 3×10⁸ × 10² = 3×10¹⁰ = 30,000,000,000 meters
