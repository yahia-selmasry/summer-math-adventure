🔢 Decimals!Fractions in disguise — just with dots!
Max, if I cut a pizza into 10 equal slices and eat 3, I ate 3/10 of it. But we can also write that as a decimal!
🍕
3/10 = 0.3?! What does that dot mean?
The dot is called the decimal point. It separates the whole number part from the fraction part!

⚠️ Common Mistake!
0.9 ≠ 0.09
0.9 = 9/10 = 90/100
0.09 = 9/100
0.9 is TEN TIMES bigger!
Always look at WHICH place the digit is in! 👀
Comparing decimals? Line up the decimal points and compare digit by digit — left to right!
