✅ Example 1 — Rounding to Nearest Thousand
"A city has 348,927 people. Round to the nearest thousand."
1 Underline: 348,927
2 Place value chart:
   3–4–8 | 9–2–7 → thousands digit = 8
3 Look one place RIGHT of thousands: 9
4 9 ≥ 5 → round UP
   348,927 → 349,000
5 Sense check: 349,000 is close to 348,927 ✓
✅ Example 2 — Place Value
"A stadium holds 2,457,830 hot dogs a season. What is the value of the digit 4?"
1 Underline: 2,457,830
2 Write out places:
   2 = millions
   4 = hundred-thousands
   5 = ten-thousands…
4 Value of 4 = 400,000
5 4 is in the 100,000s seat → 4 × 100,000 ✓
✅ Example 3 — Comparing Large Numbers
"Phoenix has 1,608,139 people. Tucson has 542,629. How many MORE people does Phoenix have?"
1 "How many MORE" = SUBTRACT
2 Line up digits by place value:
   1,608,139
   − 542,629
4 Subtract carefully
   1,608,139
   − 542,629
   = 1,065,510
5 Estimate: 1.6M − 0.5M ≈ 1.1M ✓
"How many more?"
always means
SUBTRACT!
✅ Example 4 — Ordering & Estimating
"Four schools raised money: Lincoln: $24,350 · Oak: $19,875 · Pine: $24,099 · River: $31,200. Order from greatest to least. About how much did the top two schools raise combined?"
1 Compare leading digits first:
River: 31,200 (biggest)
Lincoln: 24,350
Pine: 24,099
Oak: 19,875 (smallest)
4 Top two: River + Lincoln
Round: $31,000 + $24,000
≈ $55,000
Exact: $31,200 + $24,350 = $55,550 ✓
⚠️ CLASSIC MISTAKES
❌ Rounding the wrong digit (looking left instead of right)
❌ Forgetting to zero out digits after rounding
❌ Mixing up place value seats (thousands vs. ten-thousands)
❌ Not lining up digits when subtracting large numbers
❌ Forgetting commas make big numbers easier to read
❌ Skipping the estimate/sense-check step
💡 PRO TIPS
✓ Rounding rule: look at the digit ONE place to the RIGHT
✓ 0–4 → round DOWN, 5–9 → round UP
✓ "How many more?" → SUBTRACT the smaller from larger
✓ "About how many?" → round first, then add/subtract
✓ Use commas every 3 digits from the right
✓ Always label your answer with units!

Round each
12,450 → 12,000
8,375 → 8,000
1,200 → 1,000
Estimate: 21,000 seats
Exact total

20,825 + 1,200 = 22,025
Check
21,000 ≈ 22,025 ✓ Close!
