📏 Inequalities! Not equal — but more or less!
Max, you need at least $5 to get into the movies. Can I write that as math?
🎬 🎟️
money ≥ $5
"money is at least $5"
So $5, $6, $10… all work? That's a RANGE of answers!
The 4 Inequality Symbols:
<
Less than
x < 5: x is under 5
>
Greater than
x > 5: x is over 5
≤
Less than OR equal
x ≤ 5: x is 5 or under
≥
Greater than OR equal
x ≥ 5: x is 5 or over
Trick to remember < and >:
The open mouth always faces the bigger number! 🐊
3 < 7
"The crocodile eats the bigger number!"
🐊
Mouth → 7 (bigger!)
Solve inequalities the same way as equations — EXCEPT: flipping both sides reverses the sign!
Solve x + 4 > 9
x + 4 > 9  →  subtract 4  →  x > 5
Any number greater than 5 works: 6, 7, 8, 100…
Graph x > 5 on a number line:
→

Closed circle ● = 3 IS included (≤)
More examples:
2n < 16  →  n < 8
y − 3 ≥ 7  →  y ≥ 10
3x + 1 > 13  →  3x > 12  →  x > 4
It's just like solving equations — but the answer is a whole RANGE! 🎉
