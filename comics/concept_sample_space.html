🌳 Sample Space & Tree Diagrams List ALL possible outcomes — miss none!
Max, for lunch you can pick Turkey or Veggie AND Water or Juice. How many combos?
🥪 + 🥤 = ?
I keep losing track when I try to list them! Is there a smarter way?
A tree diagram shows every branch of choices!
START
├── Turkey
    ├── Water  Turkey + Water
    └── Juice   Turkey + Juice
└── Veggie
    ├── Water  Veggie + Water
    └── Juice   Veggie + Juice
Total: 4 outcomes
The Sample Space is the full list: {TW, TJ, VW, VJ}.
Once you have it, probability is easy!
From our 4 outcomes:
P(Turkey + Juice) = 1/4
P(any Veggie) = 2/4 = 1/2
P(any Water) = 2/4 = 1/2
Each outcome is equally likely (1/4 each)
Here's the shortcut — the Counting Principle! Just multiply the number of choices at each step!
2 sandwiches × 2 drinks = 4 total ✓
Shirts: 4 colors × 3 sizes
= 12 outfits
2 dice: 6 × 6
= 36 outcomes
3 coins: 2 × 2 × 2
= 8 outcomes
Letter (A–E) + Digit (1–5):
5 × 5 = 25 passwords
Example: Coin + Spinner (Red / Blue / Green)
H → H-R H-B H-G
T → T-R T-B T-G
Total: 2 × 3 = 6 outcomes
P(H and Red) = 1/6
The tree never misses any outcome! 🌳
