⚖️ Equations! Find the mystery number that makes both sides equal!
An expression is like a recipe. An equation says two recipes make the same thing!
Expression: 3x + 5
No = sign
↓
Equation: 3x + 5 = 20
Has an = sign! ✓
So I need to find the x that makes it TRUE!
Think of an equation like a BALANCE SCALE. Both sides must weigh the same!
x + 6 = 14
x + 6
=
14
Both sides must stay equal!
🔑 Key: Inverse Operations
+ ↔ −
opposites!
× ↔ ÷
opposites!
Whatever you do to one side,
do the same to the other!
✅ One-Step — Solve x + 6 = 14
Goal: get x alone!
x + 6 = 14
↓ subtract 6 from both sides
x = 8
✓ Check: 8 + 6 = 14 ✓
✅ Solve 4n = 28
4n = 28
↓ divide both sides by 4
n = 7
✓ Check: 4 × 7 = 28 ✓
Two-step equations need two moves. Always undo + or − first, then × or ÷!
Solve 2x + 3 = 11
2x + 3 = 11
Step 1: − 3 from both sides
2x = 8
Step 2: ÷ 2 on both sides
x = 4
✓ Check: 2(4) + 3 = 8 + 3 = 11 ✓
Word problems can turn into equations!
A cab charges $5 base + $3/mile. Total = $20. How many miles?
Write it: 3m + 5 = 20
Step 1: 3m = 15
Step 2: m = 5 miles
✓ Check: 3(5)+5=20 ✓
I think of a number, multiply by 5, subtract 8 — I get 37. What's the number?
5n − 8 = 37
5n = 45
n = 9
✓ 5(9)−8 = 45−8 = 37 ✓
Always PLUG your answer back in to check! If both sides match, you nailed it! 🎯
