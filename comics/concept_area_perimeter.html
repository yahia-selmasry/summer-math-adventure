📐 AREA & PERIMETER! Measuring Around and Inside 2D Shapes
Max, your room needs new carpet AND a fence around it! Do you know which measure helps with which?
OH! Perimeter goes
around the edge,
Area fills the inside!
📏 PERIMETER
Distance all the way AROUND the shape!
• Add up all side lengths
• Units: cm, m, ft, in (regular)
• Think: fence, picture frame, border

P = sum of all sides
⬛ AREA
Amount of space INSIDE the shape!
• Count the square units inside
• Units: cm², m², ft², in² (squared!)
• Think: carpet, paint, floor tiles

A = length × width (rectangles)
📚 FORMULA REFERENCE: All the Shapes
Rectangle
A = l × w
P = 2l + 2w
Square
A = s²
P = 4s
Triangle
A = ½ × b × h
P = a + b + c
Parallelogram
A = b × h
P = 2(a + b)
Trapezoid
A = ½(b₁ + b₂) × h    P = sum of all 4 sides
Circle
A = π × r²
C = 2 × π × r
(C = circumference,
like perimeter for circles)
π ≈ 3.14
🔢 Let's Work Some Together!
Rectangle: l=9 cm, w=4 cm
Perimeter:
P = 2(9) + 2(4)
P = 18 + 8 = 26 cm

Area:
A = 9 × 4 = 36 cm²
Triangle: b=10, h=6, sides 8,8,10
Perimeter:
P = 8 + 8 + 10 = 26 cm

Area:
A = ½ × 10 × 6
A = ½ × 60 = 30 cm²
Square: s = 7 m
Perimeter:
P = 4 × 7 = 28 m

Area:
A = 7² = 7 × 7 = 49 m²
Some shapes are made of TWO simpler shapes stuck together! Split them up and add!
L-Shape Example:
Step 1: Split into rectangles
Part A: 7 × 4 = 28
Part B: 12 × 5 = 60

Step 2: Add
Total Area = 28 + 60 = 88 square units
⚠️ COMMON MISTAKES!
❌ Forgetting to square units
Area needs cm² not cm!

❌ Using height vs. side in triangles
The height must be PERPENDICULAR to base

❌ Adding instead of halving
Triangle: A = ½ × b × h (don't forget the ½!)
