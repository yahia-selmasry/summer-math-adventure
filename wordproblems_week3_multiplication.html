ANSWER KEY

Page 1 Answers

1. 48 × 125 = 6,000 plants
2. 36 × 144 = 5,184 pencils
3. 24 × 32 = 768 seats; 768 × 15 = 11,520 audience members
4. 68 × 25 = 1,700 cents = $17.00
5. 185 ÷ 12 = 15 R5; 16 vans needed
6. 247 ÷ 8 = 30 R7; 30 full bags, 7 left over
7. 310 ÷ 9 = 34 R4; 34 complete pages, 4 photos on last page
8. 215 ÷ 18 = 11 R17; 11 prizes, 17 points remaining
Challenge: Area = 26 × 18 = 468 sq ft; 468 ÷ 12 = 39 boxes exactly

Page 2 Answers

9. Total boxes = 148 + 96 + 112 = 356; Earnings = 356 × $2 = $712
10. Wheels = 2,400 × 4 = 9,600; Packs = 9,600 ÷ 10 = 960 packs
11. Boxes = 8 × 3 × 6 = 144 boxes; Cans = 144 × 15 = 2,160 cans
12. Campers = 6 × 14 = 84; Teams = 84 ÷ 4 = 21 teams, 0 left over
13. 252 ÷ 6 = 42 (yes); 252 ÷ 8 = 31.5 (no, R4)
14. 1×36, 2×18, 3×12, 4×9, 6×6 (all factor pairs of 36)
15. 120÷4=30 ✓, 120÷5=24 ✓, 120÷6=20 ✓, 120÷7=17 R1 ✗; sizes 4, 5, 6 work
16. Possible: 8×12 (P=40), 6×16 (P=44), 4×24 (P=56); smallest P = 8×12=40 ft
Challenge: 875 ÷ 25 = 35 bars/day; 35 ÷ 5 = 7 bars/person/day; 875 = 25×35 exactly, 0 leftover
