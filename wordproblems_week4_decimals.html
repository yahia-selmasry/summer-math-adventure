ANSWER KEY

Page 1 Answers

1. Total = $8.75 + $3.49 + $5.25 = $17.49; Change = $20.00 − $17.49 = $2.51
2. Tomatoes = 3 × $2.39 = $7.17; Cucumbers = 4 × $1.15 = $4.60; Total = $11.77
3. Sale price = $34.99 − $8.50 = $26.49; Final = $26.49 − $3.25 = $23.24
4. $52.40 ÷ 4 = $13.10 per person
5. 4.75 − 1.38 = 3.37 meters
6. 3.6 + 0.85 + 0.7 = 5.15 kg
7. 12.47 − 12.09 = 0.38 seconds improvement
8. 8.5 − 3.75 = 4.75 km left; 4.75 ÷ 0.25 = 19 more days
Challenge: Small = $1.20 ÷ 0.5 = $2.40/L; Medium = $2.50 ÷ 1.25 = $2.00/L; Large = $3.80 ÷ 2 = $1.90/L → Large is best deal

Page 2 Answers

9. Fastest to slowest: Ben (61.08), Ana (62.5), Cora (62.50); Ana & Cora tied
10. Difference = 87.25 − 65.4 = 21.85 mi; Car 3h = 196.2 mi; Train 3h = 261.75 mi
11. $3.60 ÷ 12 = $0.30 per apple
12. 6-pack = $0.75/bottle; 10-pack = $0.69/bottle → 10-pack is cheaper
13. Sat = 4.5 × $9.50 = $42.75; Sun = 3 × $9.50 = $28.50; Total = $71.25
14. Total raised = $127.50 + $213.75 + $88.40 = $429.65; Still needed = $70.35
15. Spent = $25.00 − $1.75 = $23.25; One kit = $23.25 ÷ 3 = $7.75
16. Estimate: $3+$4+$3+$6+$6 = $22; Exact = $22.58
Challenge: Gallons one way ≈ 247.6 ÷ 28.5 ≈ 8.7 gal; Round trip ≈ 17.4 gal; Cost ≈ 17.4 × $3.80 ≈ $66.12
